Pancakes

Back to Homepage

These classic homemade pancakes are light, fluffy, and incredibly easy to make. Perfect for breakfast, brunch, or a sweet snack, they can be served with fresh fruit, maple syrup, powdered sugar, or your favorite toppings. This simple recipe delivers soft and golden pancakes every single time.

Ingredients for 8 pancakes:

  • 250g flour
  • 500ml milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Butter or oil for frying
  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, salt, and sugar.
  2. Add the eggs and milk gradually while stirring continuously until the batter is smooth.
  3. Stir in the melted butter and vanilla extract. Let the batter rest for about 10 minutes.
  4. Heat a lightly greased pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour a small amount of batter into the pan and spread it evenly. Cook for about 1-2 minutes until golden brown, then flip and cook the other side.
  6. Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite toppings.

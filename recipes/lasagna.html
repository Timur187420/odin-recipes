Lasagna

This classic homemade lasagna is rich, hearty, and full of comforting Italian flavors. Layers of tender pasta, savory meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese come together to create the ultimate family dinner. Perfect for special occasions or cozy evenings, this lasagna is easy to prepare and tastes even better the next day.

Ingredients for 6 servings:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 800g canned tomatoes
  • 250ml water
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp basil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 0.5 tsp black pepper
  • 250g lasagna sheets
  • 500ml béchamel sauce
  • 200g grated mozzarella cheese
  • 50g parmesan cheese
  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (top/bottom heat).
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Cook the onion until soft, then add the garlic and cook for 1 minute. Add the ground beef and brown it well.
  3. Stir in the tomato paste, canned tomatoes, water, oregano, basil, salt, and black pepper. Let the sauce simmer for about 15-20 minutes.
  4. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce. Add a layer of lasagna sheets, followed by béchamel sauce and mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used.
  5. Finish with béchamel sauce, mozzarella, and parmesan cheese on top.
  6. Bake the lasagna in the preheated oven for about 35-40 minutes until golden brown. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
